🔐 Encryptor

AES-256 encryption with password-based key derivation. Everything runs in your browser.

How it works: Your password is run through PBKDF2 (250,000 iterations, SHA-256) with a random 16-byte salt to derive a 256-bit AES key. The text is encrypted with AES-GCM using a random 12-byte IV. The salt, IV, and ciphertext are bundled together as base64 — paste it back in Decrypt mode with the same password to recover the original.
